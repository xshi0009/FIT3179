Air Pollution in India

India is one of the most air polluted countries in the world. This visualization aims to help people detected the trend of air pollution in India.

So2 in differnet states in India

In India, nearly million people are killed by the air pollution or the diseases caused by the air every year. SO2 is a kind of toxic air and it is one of the main contaminants in air.
The map shows the situation of SO2 in air in different states. The deeper color means the higher level of SO2 in air.

Air Pollution Situation In 5 Main States

This chart shows the situation of air pollution in five main states in India. It shows the three main pollution--SO2, NO2 and Respirable suspended particulate matter.
Delhi is the capital of India and Uttar Pradesh is the state has the most people in India. They are also the states that has a heavy pollution.

The change of SO2 in Uttar Pradesh From 2004 to 2010

Uttar Pradesh is the state that has the most population in India and it is also one of the air polluted states.
This line chart shows the changes of level of SO2 in different area in Uttar Pradesh.
In most areas, the level of SO2 is increasing before 2006. Since 2006, it tend to be stable or become lower in most areas.
