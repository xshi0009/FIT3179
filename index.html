Air Pollution in India

India is one of the most air polluted countries in the world. This visualization aims to help people detected the trend of air pollution in India.

So2 in differnet states in India

In India, nearly million people are killed by the air pollution or the diseases caused by the air every year. SO2 is a kind of toxic air and it is one of the main contaminants in air.
The map shows the situation of SO2 in air in different states. The deeper color means the higher level of SO2 in air.
